Welcome to Simple Sushi!

Simple Sushi is a website you can browse to look for easy yet delicious recipes of any kind of sushi. We collect these recipes online and bring them to you quicker than just surfing through the internet to find a decent recipe. With Simple Sushi, you can whip up a batch of your favorite sushi in a few minutes! All you gotta do is search for the sushi (using the search bar), click on the most recommended, and read from there! To assist anyone tryna make their sushi just in the comfort of their own home, spread and share this website so everyone can enjoy sushi from their own kitchen and table.
